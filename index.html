    • I'm Ashvik, a 16-year-old filmmaker from Hyderabad, India. From a young age, I've had a passion for creating stories and bringing them to life through the medium of film. It all began when I was just a child, and I discovered the magical world of movies. However, what truly captivated me was not just watching films, but understanding the intricate process behind their creation.

    As a child, I would often find myself immersed in the world of storytelling. At the young age of 6, I started crafting my own tales, fueled by my vivid imagination and a desire to share my narratives with others. Initially, my primary audience consisted of my parents, who always lent a patient ear to my imaginative tales. Occasionally, I would also gather my friends around, eager to captivate them with the stories that unfolded in my mind.

    I immersed into the art of creating films since I was 10 and have continued to pursue the hobby for over half a decade, creating almost a dozen short films.

    I've also written multiple screenplays, with a few winning awards as well.
